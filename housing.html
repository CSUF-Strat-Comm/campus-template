Living on Campus

Living on campus should feel like your home away from home. There are many ways to connect with people, places, and the community. Housing and Residential Engagement is here to help this happen.

Take a Tour

Our staff is happy to show model rooms to potential students and their families. Just stop by our office during our daily drop-in tour times - no reservation required - or schedule a group tour for a group of 20 or more.

Apply for Housing

Apply for student housing for the 2020-2021 Academic Year. To log in to the Housing Application Portal, use your Cal State Fullerton username and password and then click the Application Tab to access the application.

Dining and Catering

The Gastronome is a unique, state of the art dining facility. It provides the CSUF community a great opportunity to not only eat a healthy and nutritious meal, but it also serves as a hub of activity for the community.
