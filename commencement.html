Frequently Asked Questions

Registration

What is the difference between Commencement and Graduation?

Graduation is the completion of all degree requirements. Commencement is the ceremony that celebrates the completion of a degree. Graduation questions? Visit: http://records.fullerton.edu/academics/graduation.php

Still need to apply for graduation?

Contact the Graduation Unit.

Do you have questions about your TDA (Titan Degree Audit)?

Contact a Graduation Specialist.

What if I finished school in December?

Although classes end in December, your degree is conferred in 2022 which makes you a member of the Class of 2022! All grads are invited to participate in the May 2022 ceremonies. Continue to check your csu.fullerton.edu email and this website for important info and updates.
